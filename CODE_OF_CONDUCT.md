# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as maintainers and contributors pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Positive behaviors include:
- Being kind and welcoming to all participants
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback graciously
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behaviors include:
- Harassment or intimidation in any form
- Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional in a collaborative setting
